Why are these children out of School?
Understanding the Sub-Saharan Child
Some of the purest coutries in the world struggle to finance an education system for all their children. Families in villages cannot afford the tuition, books and other fees. This poverty means that children lack the daily nutritous meals and access to clean water The prevailant discrimination of girls by legal or religious reasons continues to add to the list of children not in school.
